Agriculture Robot

(An approach to Automation in agriculture)

Team: A Ajay Joshua, Sanjay R, Dhilip H

Guide: V.Sridharan (CEO of FIRST-TBI, SASTRA)

Problem Statement

Agriculture as a field involves a large array of operations which require a complete set of skills to yield better outputs

However, as we are in need of automation we require the field of robotics to bear such responsibilities. Hence our main aim is to integrate many operations that can be done by a single module

Existing Models

Methodology

Our project focuses on, making use of fixed rail arrangement to house the robot module and also facilitate the movement by providing a flat arrangement for its movement analogous to the monorail

And the module is designed to perform various operations of agricultural practice. For the scope of mini project this module is limited to perform seeding and weeding operations

Our Works

  • To design a kinematic mechanism to perform seeding and weeding
  • To perform Structural and Kinematic simulations and Analysis using ANSYS & ADAMS
  • To design a fixed rail arrangement to facilitate the movement of the module
